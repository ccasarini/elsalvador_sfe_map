It can be a loop, but we donít have to use it in circle:- Define: what question are you trying to answer?- Find: what data exists that could help?- Get: how do you acquire that data?- Verify: can you trust what you got?- Clean: is it in a usable shape?- Analyse: what does it tell you?- Present: how do you communicate the findings? If you want to use AI to gather data, we have prepared ourselves to put some boundaries and using the data pipeline can help to not fall in the ØmagicÆ of AI.Definition of data for me --> numbers but also facts, countering of facts but also analysis of numbers and facts.Lecture 2 - 12th March 2026Data pipeline: Structure the thinking of how to deliver data workFrom Problem Definition to Data CollectionExample: how OpenElenctions standardizes US election data (https://thescoop.org/archives/2025/06/09/how-openelections-uses-llms/ )The OpenElenctions project collects, cleans and publishes election results from all 50 US states in a single standardized format.
